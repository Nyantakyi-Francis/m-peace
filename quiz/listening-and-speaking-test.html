Before you begin

Make sure you are in a quiet place, your internet connection is stable, and you have your correct student details ready before opening the assessment.

  • 1
    Prepare your details Enter your full name, index number, and class exactly as required before starting.
  • 2
    One question at a time You cannot skip a question, and each answer locks once selected.
  • 3
    Do not leave the page Refreshing the page, switching tabs, or exiting fullscreen may be logged during the assessment.
  • 4
    Submit only once Each index number is allowed only one completed submission.
Important: Click Start Quiz only when you are ready. The quiz opens in a new tab so you can keep this page as your reference point.
