Executives

Meet the dedicated leaders guiding our foundation

Our Leadership Team

A team of dedicated professionals committed to giving the female a voice in rural Ghana.

Mprah Ruhaimatu

Executive Director & Founder

In 2020, Mprah Ruhaimatu established the Marvelous Touch Foundation (MATOF) with the visionary goal of amplifying the female voice across Ghana. Her work is deeply rooted in the belief that empowering women is the catalyst for broader societal transformation. As a dedicated gender activist, Ruhaimatu has positioned herself as a beacon of hope for marginalized women in rural communities. By serving as an inspirational speaker, she bridges the gap between advocacy and action, providing these women with the tools and confidence to overcome systemic barriers. Beyond her grassroots activism, Ruhaimatu is a distinguished academic whose intellectual pursuits fuel her mission for social equity. She currently serves as a lecturer at Mampong Technical College of Education, where she shapes the minds of future educators. Her commitment to excellence is further evidenced by her status as a Ph.D. candidate in English Language at the University of Education, Winneba.

Sandra Nunya McBilly

General Secretary

Sandra Nunya McBilly serves as the Secretary of Marvelous Touch Foundation, where she plays a key role in supporting the organization's day-to-day operations and long-term mission. With a strong commitment to service and organization, Sandra ensures the foundation's administrative functions run smoothly and transparently. Sandra manages official correspondence, maintains records and documentation, coordinates meetings, and supports the Board and management team in governance and compliance matters. Her active attention to detail and reliability help uphold the foundation’s values of accountability and integrity. Beyond administrative responsibilities, Sandra is deeply passionate about the foundation's cause and takes pride in contributing to initiatives that create meaningful, positive impact in the community. Her dedication, professionalism, and collaborative spirit make her an essential part of the foundation’s work.

Matieyenu Mathew Yenugbate

Public Relations Officer

I am a purpose-driven student leader, peer counsellor, and graphic designer with a strong interest in mental health advocacy, youth development, and effective communication. I serve as the Public Relations Officer (PRO) for Marvelous Touch Foundation (NGO), where I manage public engagement, visual communication, and awareness initiatives using both strategic messaging and creative design. I also serve as a Peer Counsellor at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), supporting student wellbeing through mentorship and guidance. I value empathy, creativity, and collaboration.

Empowering marginalized girls and women in rural communities through education and advocacy.
